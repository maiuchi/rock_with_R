Welcome!

Thank you so much for joining the workshop today!


This workshop is for absolute beginners who have no prior experience in R and/ or coding.

After this workshop, participants will be able to:

  • Evaluate whether R is the appropriate tool for their project
  • Distinguish between exploratory and explanatory visualization
  • Examine data distribution using a graphic tool
  • Create a simple data visualization piece using R
